Understanding Your Property Tax Changes

Wake County has recently undergone a property revaluation process. This process aims to redistribute the existing property tax burden based on the updated property values. In addition to this, new annual budgets have been passed, which include adjustments to tax rates. These changes can have varying impacts on your property tax bill depending on the specifics of your property and the town you live in.

Use this tool to understand how these changes affect your property tax bill. Simply enter your previous and new property valuations, select your town, and we’ll break down the impact for you.
